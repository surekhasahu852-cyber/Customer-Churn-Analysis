# Customer-Churn-Analysis
PURPOSE 
The main goal of this project is to understand why customers are leaving the company (churning). By analyzing this data, businesses can find out which regions, genders, or signup periods have the highest churn rates and take quick actions to retain their customers.
Tools Used: Power BI (for data cleaning, modeling, and dashboard creation)
DASHBOARD FEATURES
Key Metrics (KPIs): Displays total customers (50K), churned customers (14K), active customers (36K), and the overall churn rate (29%) right at the top.Interactive Filters: Side filters for Signup Quarter, Gender, and Country to drill down into specific data.Visual Breakdown: Uses bar charts, donut charts, and line graphs to show churn trends clearly.
Key Insights
Top Country for Churn: The USA has the highest number of churned customers (5.1K), followed by the UK (2.2K).Gender Distribution: The customer base is almost equally divided between Males (50.23%) and Females (47.89%).Login Frequency Impact: Customers who log in very less frequently (close to 0) show a massive spike in churn.Membership Duration: The churn rate fluctuates between 26% and 30% over the years, peaking around the 6th year of membership.
